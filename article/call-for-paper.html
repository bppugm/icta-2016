Call for Paper

Dear researchers,

It is our pleasure to call for your participation to this unique conference to share your frontier works with other researchers. We are sure that through this conference, you will find many peers to share ideas and discuss future researchs. We also encourage graduate students, both M.Sc. and Ph.D. students, to submit your thesis works to this conference.

The conference organize plenary lectures, invited lectures, and general contributions. We have five symposia in this conference, that are Bio-prospecting, Computer Science, Nanoscience and Chemistry, Physics and Its Applications, and Geo-Maritime.

The submitted papers will be reviewed by at least two international peer reviewers. The accepted papers will be published in the journal and proceedings, i.e. Biological Research Journal (Pagepress), IEEE Xplore digital library, Materials Science Forum, Indonesian Journal of Chemistry, AIP Conference Proceeding, and Indonesian Journal of Geography. These publishing companies are indexed by Scopus. Those who do not wish to have full paper submission, we can accept extended abstract.

Please let your colleagues know about this conference.

Guideline for preparation of full paper

Your paper must be strictly formatted using a Camera Ready Copy (CRC) MS Word or LaTeX according to the template provided each symposia. Figures and tables should be embedded and not supplied separately. Paper template for each symposium can be downloaded in the symposia page.

Guideline for submission of full paper

Submission and registration include payment of fees will be absolutely necessary for attending any of the conference events. To ensure that your submission and registration include payment information will be processed quickly and correctly, the organizers request that you submit using ePaper Management.
